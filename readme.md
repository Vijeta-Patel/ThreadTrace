# Thread-Trace

ThreadTrace is a project that helps people check whether a garment is genuine and learn where it comes from. It gives each product a digital identity so brands, artisans, and customers can verify important details with a simple QR code scan.

The idea behind ThreadTrace is to make fashion more transparent and trustworthy. It is especially useful for protecting original designs, reducing fake products, and helping small textile brands show proof of authenticity in a simple and affordable way.

## What it does

- Gives every garment its own digital record
- Lets users scan a QR code to check product authenticity
- Helps track product origin and manufacturing details
- Supports trust, transparency, and better product traceability

## Why it matters

Counterfeit clothing and unclear product information are major challenges in the textile industry. Thread-Trace aims to make it easier for customers to trust what they buy, while also helping brands and artisans protect their work and prepare for growing global transparency requirements.

## Project goal

This project is built as a practical step toward a Digital Product Passport system for garments, with a focus on authenticity, transparency, and support for the textile industry.
